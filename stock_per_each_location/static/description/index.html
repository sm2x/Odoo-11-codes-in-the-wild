Stock Per Each Location

This module helps user to manage the stock of the particular product,its forecasting, incoming products and outgoing products.

Inventory > Settings > Warehouse > Enable Multi-Warehouses

Settings > Users and companies > Users > Technical settings -> Enable Manage Multiple Stock Locations

Settings > Users and companies > Users > Technical settings > Enable Manage Multiple Warehouses

Settings > Users and companies > Users -> Technical settings -> Enable Manage Product Variants

  • After installing the module you will get the table named "Stock Balance By Location" in Inventory -> Products -> Inventory(Page).In this table warehouse location is displayed for that particular product , how much quantity it has right now, its forecasting, incoming products and outgoing products.
  • If you want to directly update the quantity you can directly update it by clicking on the "Update Qty On Hand" but for that product type should be stockable product.There product name will be displayed, we have select the particular location of warehouse and how much quantity we want to update.After selecting both the things when you will click on apply it will update its quantity on that particular location.
  • For updating the Incoming products you have to create the purchase order for that particular product.At that time in its "Deliveries and Invoices(page)" you have to check the warehouse and in that default destination location.After confirming the purchase order incoming quantity will be increased in that products in particular location and if you validated that in shipment it will be updated in onhand quantity.
  • For updating the Outgoing products you have to create the sales order for that particular product.Before confirming sales order you have to check the warehouse and its rules.From that warehouse only your products will be picked up and it will come in outgoing products and after validating it in shipment it will be deducted. .
  • You can make the report of particular product in print-> Stock Location.
  • You can also make the report of multiple products in print-> Stock Location.
