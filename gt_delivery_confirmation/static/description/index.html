Send Email Notification for complete delivery orders to customers.

Notification plays an important role when you send any kind of product to your customer – it enhances the customer satisfaction that the product is in the delivery stages and provides your organization to maintain the customers properly with greater satisfaction. With the same we have crafted this module that helps your customers to get email when the delivery of your orders and products are confirmed by you Company.

Before buying want to see demo contact us

Features of this module

  • Send automatic email notification to customers
  • Send email when the order is confirmed
  • Sending emails in bulk
  • Configure warehouse wise for sending emails
  • Select from the operation – Single email, bulk email or send email manually.

Let’s have a look how the module work with Odoo

After installation of Odoo email notification for delivery order you can go into the settings part Click on inventory > Warehouse Management > Warehouses


There you can configure Send delivery email and send automatic email
